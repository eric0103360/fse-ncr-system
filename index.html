Step-by-Step Detail
Step 1
Raise a Non-Conformance
👤 Anyone — No password needed
Anyone who identifies a non-conformance can raise an NCR directly from the login screen.
  • Click "Raise a Non-Conformance" on the login screen
  • Fill in your name, date, issue type and department
  • Select priority — Critical, Major or Minor
  • Set a suggested due date and responsible person
  • Describe the non-conformance in detail
  • Submit — you receive a reference number
⏱ Takes about 3–5 minutes
Step 2
Notify the Responsible Person
👤 Originator
After submitting, notify the person who needs to action it and also inform the Quality Manager.
  • Use the 💬 WhatsApp button — message pre-filled automatically
  • Or use the 📧 Email button — email pre-filled automatically
  • Share the reference number e.g. PND-847291
  • Also notify the Quality Manager (Admin) to review
⏱ Immediately after submitting
Step 3
Admin Reviews & Approves
⚙️ Admin Only
The Quality Manager reviews every submission before it becomes an official NCR.
  • Admin logs in — alert popup shows pending items immediately
  • Reviews submission in ⏳ Pending Review tab
  • Can adjust priority, due date and assignee
  • Click ✅ Accept → becomes official NCR with number
  • Click ❌ Decline → removed (duplicate / not valid)
  • Sends WhatsApp or Email to assignee with full NCR details
⏱ Within 24 hours of submission
Step 4
Assignee Completes Actions
✏️ Assignee — Password: FSE.Assign01
The responsible person logs in and completes the root cause analysis and corrective actions.
  • Open the link sent by Admin
  • Login with password: FSE.Assign01
  • Find your NCR → click Complete Actions
  • Fill in Root Cause — 5 Why analysis
  • Fill in Corrective Action taken
  • Fill in Preventive Action to avoid recurrence
  • Tick the declaration, type your name → Submit & Sign Off
⏱ Must be done before due date
Step 5
Admin Approves or Rejects
⚙️ Admin Only
Admin reviews the completed actions and either closes or rejects the NCR.
  • Admin sees "Awaiting Approval" alert on login
  • Opens the NCR and reviews all completed actions
  • Click ✅ Approve & Close — NCR is officially closed
  • Click ❌ Reject — provide reason, returns to assignee for rework
⏱ Within 48 hours of assignee submission
Step 6
NCR Closed & Recorded
🔒 System — Automatic
Once approved the NCR is permanently recorded in the system for audit purposes.
  • Status changes to ✔ Closed with date and sign-off
  • Visible in NCR Register permanently
  • Repeat patterns tracked automatically
  • Charts and dashboards update instantly
  • Printable report available at any time
⏱ Permanent record
